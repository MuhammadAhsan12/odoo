Who We Are?

AQAHI is a company specializing in coffee business and coffeeshops services since 2001. We provide a wide variety of services in coffee field including providing consultations, planning, designing, implementing, processing, and training courses. In addition, we supply all equipment, materials and operational products, operation follow-up, and quality control by authorized experts in coffee field to best serve our customers of government departments, private sectors, coffee shop companies, and personal coffeeshops. We import the finest coffee since 2006 directly from different farms and sources in addition to providing a wide variety of specialty coffee for roasteries and local markets from different countries. About The Founder

Dr. AbdulKarim Al Ojaimi was born in Hail and completed his general education therein. He received His first bachelor’s degree in Anesthesiology from the university of Qassim. Following that, He received his second bachelor’s degree in Science of Anesthesiology and Surgical Practices from the university of Cardiff based in UK. This was followed by receiving bachelor’s degree of Health and Social Care Studies from the University of Bolton based in UK.
