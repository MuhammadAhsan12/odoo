About Us

AQAHI is a company specializing in coffee business and coffeeshops services since 2001. We provide a wide variety of services in coffee field including providing consultations, planning, designing, implementing, processing, and training courses. In addition, we supply all equipment, materials and operational products, operation follow-up, and quality control by authorized experts in coffee field to best serve our customers of government departments, private sectors, coffee shop companies, and personal coffeeshops.
